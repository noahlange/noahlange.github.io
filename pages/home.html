Noah Lange is a programmer, print and web designer and musician based out of Green Bay, Wisconsin.

The resident print designer and manager of the award-winning website for the Luther College student newspaper Chips, Lange has interests ranging from print design and layout to web design and programming, as well as music performance, composition and production.

Lange graduated cum laude from Luther College in the spring of 2014 with a double major in English and Computer Science.
